An Ode to Cake

There is a delicacy called "Cake"
A Fluffy dessert that goes in the oven
Cake is something one must bake
A very close relative to a not so delicious muffin.
A cake is usually served on ones birthday
Cake tastes best with icing
One can even dress the cake with candles
After the candles are blown it's time for slicing
The utensils for slicing have handles Now it's time to eat
